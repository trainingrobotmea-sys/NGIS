## Quick reference
- **Brand:** NextGen International School (NGIS) — international school, ages 3–18. Tagline: "Transforming Education for Better Tomorrow." Tone: serious, academic, professional. No emoji, no hype.
- **Colors:** Red `#e41e27` (primary), Navy `#183763` (anchor), Cerulean `#2394be`, Green `#4ab84b`, Gold `#fdcc0e`. Cool slate neutrals.
- **Type:** Spectral (serif) for display/headings; Mulish (sans) for UI/body.
- **Tokens:** link `styles.css` for all CSS custom properties.
- **Components:** `components/core/` — Button, IconButton, Badge, Tag, Card, Stat, EyebrowLabel, Input, Select, Alert, Avatar.
- **UI kit:** `ui_kits/website/` — full marketing-site recreation.
- **Templates:** `templates/website-page/` — copy-to-start branded homepage.
- **Logo:** `assets/ngis-logo-full.jpeg`.

See `readme.md` for the full content, visual, and iconography guidelines.
