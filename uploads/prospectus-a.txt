OUR
FOUNDERʼS
MESSAGE
With warm regards,
Ibrahim Askani
NextGen International School
Dear Parents and Guardians,
It fills me with immense pride and humility to welcome you to NextGen International School, a
school born from a vision to transform education. As we look at the world our children are
inheriting, one shaped by artificial intelligence, rapid change, and global challenges, we asked
ourselves if traditional schooling is enough. The honest answer was no, and that realization led
to the creation of NGIS.
NGIS is the first ETM-powered school in Karachi, built on South Korean educational standards
and grounded in Islamic values. We are committed to equipping students with the skills,
knowledge, 	and 	mindset 	needed 	to 	succeed 	in 	an 	ever-evolving 	world 	while 	nurturing
curiosity, confidence, and a strong sense of purpose.
Above all, your childʼs safety and wellbeing are our highest priority. From the moment they
enter our campus to the time they return home, every measure is designed to ensure they feel
secure 	and 	supported. 	We 	are 	not 	just 	educating 	children, 	we 	are 	shaping 	confident,
compassionate individuals who are ready to make a positive impact on the world, Inshallah.

OUR
DIRECTORʼS
MESSAGE
Dear Parents and Guardians,
NextGen International School was not created to be just another name among Pakistanʼs
schools. It was built to answer a question every thoughtful parent is asking today: in a world
shaped by technology, faith, and global competition, is traditional schooling still enough? Our
answer is no, and NGIS is our response. As An Initiative of Robotmea, powered by ETM on South
Korean educational standards and accredited by Robotron, South Korea, we bring a globally
benchmarked system to Karachi, grounded in Islamic values and delivered through a modern
STEAM framework.
To our parents, your childʼs safety, character, faith, and intellect are not separate priorities,
they are our entire purpose. To our students, be bold, stay curious, and take pride in who you
are, because the future belongs to those who are ready to build it.
This is Pakistanʼs first ETM-powered school, a shift from transactional to transformational
education. We warmly invite you to be part of this journey, InshaAllah.
With warm regards,
Saif-ur-Rehman
